# Lumen Office RPG

A **mysterious office RPG** playable directly in your browser. Explore a corporate office, uncover secrets, solve puzzles, and survive while managing your sanity, memory, and suspicion.  

Built entirely with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** — no backend required. Simply open the HTML file in any modern browser to play.

---

**Features**

- **Branching Narrative** – Multiple endings based on your choices.  
- **Mini Puzzles** – Unlock doors with codes and decode files to advance.  
- **Sanity & Memory Mechanics** – Low sanity blurs text and scrambles options, increasing challenge.  
- **Randomized Secrets** – Key items (like Keycards, USB drives, encrypted notes) appear in different locations each playthrough.  
- **Inventory System** – Track collected items in your adventure.  
- **Flickering Office Lights & Effects** – Occasional flickers for immersive atmosphere.  
- **Restart Button** – Restart the game at the end without refreshing the page.  

---

**How to Play**

1. **Open the Game**  
   - Download the HTML file (`lumen_office_rpg.html`) from this repository.  
   - Open it in **Chrome, Firefox, or Edge**.

2. **Read Instructions**  
   - The game includes a visible **instructions panel** at the top with tips for managing Sanity, Memory, and Suspicion.  
   - Pay attention to puzzles and secret items to maintain your stats.

3. **Make Choices**  
   - Click on the buttons in the options panel to progress the story.  
   - Choices affect your **Memory**, **Suspicion**, and **Sanity** stats.

4. **Solve Puzzles**  
   - Door codes and file ciphers must be solved to unlock certain paths.  
   - Incorrect attempts decrease sanity.

5. **Collect Secrets**  
   - Key items are needed to unlock hidden areas or achieve the best endings.  

6. **Endings**  
   - The story has **multiple endings**:  
     - **Freedom** – Escape successfully.  
     - **Captured** – You are caught exploring.  
     - **Mastermind** – Successfully uncover all secrets.  
     - **Forgotten** – Ignore everything and continue routine work.  
   - Restart the game at the end using the **Restart Game** button.

---

**Stats Explained**

- **Memory** – Increases when discovering clues and solving puzzles; needed to access secrets.  
- **Suspicion** – Increases when taking risky actions; high suspicion may limit choices.  
- **Sanity** – Decreases from mistakes or stressful events; low sanity blurs and scrambles text.
